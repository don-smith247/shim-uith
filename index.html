U.I.T.H,Health Information Management Programs

HND Program

Our HND (Higher National Diploma) program in Health Information Management provides a comprehensive education in the field of healthcare data management. Students will develop skills in data analysis, health informatics, and medical coding.

ND Program

The ND (National Diploma) program in Health Information Management offers a practical, hands-on approach to healthcare data management. Students will learn about healthcare administration, medical terminology, and electronic health record systems.
